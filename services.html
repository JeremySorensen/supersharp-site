Get the Right Cut Every Time With Our Knife Sharpening Services

If it has a blade, Super Sharp can sharpen it. Based in Folsom, California, we provide meticulous tool blade, scissor, and knife sharpening services. We make sharpening your blades easy and convenient by offering on-site services, working around your schedule and coming to you so the job gets done faster.

Our professionals will sharpen all types of tools and blades, ranging in length from 1″ to 20″. We will get the job done. Our services are available for the following:

  • Kitchen Knives
  • Ceramic Knives
  • Serrated Knives
  • Pizza Cutters
  • Pocket/Hunting Knives
  • Pruners
  • Salon Shears
  • Groomer Shears
  • Scissors Fabric/Paper
  • Lopers
  • Hedge Shears
  • Axes/Hatchets/Machetes
  • Shop Tools

Technology

We use the latest and greatest wet stone sharpening technology. Wet grinding is a high tech, low heat, environmentally friendly sharpening system. Our professionals are extremely knowledgeable about the skills and techniques involved in sharpening blades, and our final product is always top-notch.
